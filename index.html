About me

Who am I?

Hello, my name is Jakub Drozdowski and I’m a 23-year-old full-stack web developer. I was born in Nowy Sącz but currently I live in Crakow. I finished technical secondary school with specialisation in informatics where my enthusiasm for programming and desire to create has been born. Recently I graduated at Jagiellonian University with a degree in Bachelor of Electronic Information Processing where I gained necessary knowledge and discovered a passion for building useful and ease on the eye things.

I enjoy creating meaningful projects and taking great satisfaction from them.

I'm not afraid of challenges. I'm persistent in pursuing a goal and willingly and quickly learn new things. My meticulous attention to detail significantly enhances my analytical skills. I possess the ability to adapt to new situations and feel comfortable working in a team.

Obviously I like watching movies, reading books and listening to music. But also I'm very keen on sport, psychology and design.
